Testing Guide: BUG-V01
======================
Kanban exposes all sales to vendedora


WHAT THE CLIENT REPORTED:
-------------------------
When a vendedora logs in and opens the Kanban page, they could see ALL 16 sales
from ALL sellers (user_ids 1, 3, 5), not just their own sales.
This is a data isolation failure.


WHAT WAS FIXED:
---------------
Now vendedoras can only see their own sales on Kanban.
Admins/financeiros can still see all sales.


HOW TO TEST:
------------

Step 1: Open your browser
   Go to: https://bbkit.onrender.com

Step 2: Login as VENDEDORA
   - Email: bbkits@hotmail.com
   - Password: 12345678
   - Click "Entrar" button

Step 3: Click "Kanban" in the navigation menu
   - Look at the top navigation bar
   - You will see: Dashboard | Vendas | Kanban
   - Click "Kanban"

Step 4: Check the Kanban board
   - Count how many sales cards appear
   - You should ONLY see YOUR OWN sales
   - You should NOT see sales from other sellers

Step 5: Login as ADMIN to compare
   - Click your name in top right corner
   - Click "Sair" (logout)
   - Login as admin:
     Email: admin@bbkits.com
     Password: (admin password)
   - Click "Kanban" in navigation
   - Admin should see ALL sales from all sellers


EXPECTED RESULT:
----------------
- Vendedora sees ONLY their own sales (1-2 sales)
- Admin sees ALL sales (16 sales)


NAVIGATION:
-----------
Top menu -> Click "Kanban" button
